Rebecca Made is a fun little collection of things that Rebecca Snyder has made.

Rebecca earned her Bachelor of Arts degree in graphic design and business from Brigham Young University, and grew up in the most beautiful state in America: Utah. She has since ventured to Dallas to work for Click Here Labs, part of The Richards Group, as a front-end web developer by day, and graphic designer by night.
