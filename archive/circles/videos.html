In college, I was able to take a class about motion graphic videos. I learned how to use After Effects and designed the imagery and text for both of the following videos.

Times & Seasons

Using only text and dingbats found on standard fonts, I created this video to highlight one of my favorite scriptures. I wanted to add personality to the text without using any imagery. Emphasis was put on simplicity and sound design.
Music by Yann Tiersen from "Amelie"




Garden Club of America Promotional Commercial

I had to animate a story for a non-profit organization of choice with a time limit of only 30 seconds. I wanted to show how simple it is to plant a garden and illustrate the benefits that come from a personal garden. Music by The Weepies
